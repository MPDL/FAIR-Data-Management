# Contributing to the FAIR Research Data Management Workshop

Want to help improve our workshop website? We welcome contributions from the community! This document outlines how to propose updates, contributions, and improvements to our workshop materials.
Contributions are guided by the principles of transparency, consensus, and alignment with the FAIR principles.

---

## How to Contribute

- **Direct GitHub Submissions:**  
  - Propose changes by creating pull requests in this GitHub project.

- **Email Submissions:**  
  - You can also reach out via [rdm@maxit.mpg.de](mailto:rdm@maxit.mpg.de).

- **In Case of Uncertainty:**  
  - The group of creators is available to consult with you to clarify any ambiguities and help reach consensus.

---

Your contributions help maintain the integrity and relevance of our workshop materials.
Thank you for your engagement and dedication to fostering FAIR Research Data Management.
